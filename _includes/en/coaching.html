Coaching & Supervision

Coaching

My coaching services support professionals, leaders, and teams in navigating work-related challenges and achieving their professional or personal goals. Through structured, resource- and solution- focused methods, I support you in focusing on your individual strengths and resources as well as nding solutions to complex problems.

Promoting self-efficacy is my guiding principle here.

Supervision

Supervision provides teams with the opportunity to reflect on professional experiences and work- related challenges. In a safe environment, I support you in developing strategies for conflict resolution and reflecting on team collaboration, or addressing stress factors that impact your work environment.

This service is especially aimed at professionals in social, educational, and therapeutic fields, as well as teams within organizations seeking to strengthen their approach to team dynamics.
