Therapy

Systemic Therapy

I offer systemic therapy for individuals who are facing a crisis, dealing with personal or relationship conflicts, feeling overwhelmed, or wanting to grow personally. I help you to develop new perspectives and encourage a more supportive view of yourself.

Couples Therapy

I work with couples who want to improve their relationship or work through conflicts. In couples therapy, we often focus on patterns of communication and conflict in the relationship. I help you to recognize these patterns and develop a new understanding of each other.

Family Therapy

For families going through challenging times, I offer family therapy. With my experience in intensive family support and family therapy (SGB VIII), along with parent coaching based on Haim Omer's methods, I support families dealing with conflicts, crises, or separation.
